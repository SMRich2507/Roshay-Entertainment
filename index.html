The Artist

Hip Hop Rap Salsa Bachata

Born with the rhythm of Puerto Rico in his veins, Roshay brings an electrifying fusion of hip hop, rap, salsa, and bachata to every stage he touches. His music is a love letter to the island's streets and sounds, blending raw lyrical energy with Caribbean soul. Every performance is a full-body experience that leaves audiences moving, feeling, and wanting more.

The Instructor

Salsa Bachata Hip Hop

Roshay teaches dance the way he lives it, with passion, patience, and an infectious energy that makes everyone feel like they belong on the floor. Whether you've never danced a step or you've been moving for years, his classes are built to meet you where you are and push you further. Community first, technique always, ego never.
